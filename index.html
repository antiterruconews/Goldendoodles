About Me

Hi! I'm Angie, and my passion for Goldendoodles started over 5 years ago. As a dedicated breeder, I maintain 3 to 4 litters per year, ensuring each puppy receives the love and care they deserve.

My breeding dogs are carefully selected for their temperament, health, and physical characteristics. All are certified clear for hip and elbow dysplasia, and have all necessary genetic testing completed.

Each puppy is socialized from an early age and comes with a health guarantee, first vaccinations, and complete deworming. My commitment is to find the perfect family for each of my babies.

I also provide services including coaching sessions for new breeders and general consulting for dog owners.
